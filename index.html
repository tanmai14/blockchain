About Us

ReDapp (Retail Distributed Application) is our effort to bring a blockchain based implementation for the traditional, present day supply chain management. All the key stakeholders involved in the system like the Manufacturer, Distributor, Retailer and the End customer can make use of this portal so that the entire chain of supply is transparent, end to end traceable and hence more trustworthy.
